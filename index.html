Introduction

Dragonball Legends is a gacha game with role playing gameplay and fighting game mechanics. In this game it requires players to assemble teams of fighters in the form of z power through the various game modes like short-time quests, story and upgrade.

Game Modes

Short-Time Quests: Players here are able to unlock special charaters and items.

Story: Allows you to play through the arc of original series.

Upgrade: Upgrading consists of various battles to quickly upgrade your fighters.
